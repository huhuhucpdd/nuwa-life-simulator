## Abilities at a Glance

- **Timeline Editor**  
  Not a passive test. An active game. Say: “Go back to June 2023, when I turned down that overseas job. What if I had said yes?”

- **Self-Distillation Engine**  
  Feed in your chat logs, social media data, or just talk. The system extracts your behavioral patterns inside a privacy sandbox — and automatically corrects self-enhancement bias.

- **Quantum Life Simulator**  
  Based on your real traits and a large anonymous path database, three timelines (α / β / γ) are generated using a hybrid engine (statistical model + personal deviation + randomness).

- **Event Deck**  
  Trigger random life events: “industry black swan”, “a mentor appears”… add a dramatic twist to your simulation.

- **Parallel Universe Cards**  
  Compare your income, mood, and freedom 5 years later across two timelines with a single command.

- **100% Skip Rights**  
  The question bank is massive, but you can skip any question, any question type, or all questions — and run with default traits immediately.
